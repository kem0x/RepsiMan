Pepsiman,
recompiled.

Play Pepsiman online in a modern browser through a WebAssembly-first static recompilation of the original PlayStation game, with optional visual and quality-of-life features. Bring your own legally obtained Pepsiman disc files; the open-source BIOS is included.

Built with PSXRecomp. Static recompilation translates the original executable for modern hardware; unlike a full source-code decompilation, it does not recreate the game as a conventional source port.

Modern picture16:9, corrected textures, and optional geometry correction.
Smoother play60 FPS presentation, faster loading, and low-latency input.
Built for browsersControllers, remapping, persistent saves, fullscreen, and offline play.
